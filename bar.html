Hall interior designs with pendant lights

Pendant lights make the space appear larger and more opulent. These lights also give the impression that the ceiling is higher. To create an elegant and modern environment, the area all around light can be further decorated with modern wall decor and fancy pots.

Hall interior designs with artwork

Adding drawings to hall interior designs is the simplest way to add colour and charm that is most reflective of your style. The key to choosing suitable artwork is that it should be appropriate for the hall. For example, choose a traditional and cultural painting if your hall has traditional decor, such as a wooden swing and earthen pots. If the hall is modern and minimalistic, contemporary and abstract art can be chosen.

FORMAL LIVING AREA
