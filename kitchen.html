TRADITIONAL KITCHEN

There has always been grace and elegance in traditional kitchens! The traditional kitchen layout has been around for quite some time, but as homeowners search for ways to get more from their cooking spaces, classic simplicity with a dash of modern flair has emerged as the new design mantra.

A-U shaped modren kitchen

A U-shaped kitchen, also known as a horseshoe kitchen, is a common kitchen layout that's efficient and versatile. It features built-in cabinets, countertops, and appliances on three walls, with the fourth side left open or featuring an entry door. The U-shape can free up floor space and make it easier to access different areas of the kitchen without walking across the room.
